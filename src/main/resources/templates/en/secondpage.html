Step two: Arrange the bag

Going to university without a bag or with an empty bag is like hockey without ice or basketball without baskets. It just can't happen.
So the second step is to look at the schedule for the day and arrange the bag with the necessary things.

Necessary things

1) Computer - no way without it. You can look at the materials that have been placed in the e-studijas, write down the things that the teacher has said, or just relax in the meantime while playing a game.

2) Notepads - even if I have a computer, I can often write things down faster in a notebook, because I often have to draw different diagrams, which would take more time on a computer than in a notebook.

3) Pen and pencil - the main tools to be able to write down information in a notebook, if it is not up, then you have to ask a colleague. But if there is, then I always take up several so I can help colleagues if they have forgotten.

4) Water and a snack - water is the main ingredient for us to exist at all, so I take water and a snack every time, if we really want to eat something before or after lunch.
