From home to university

For each of us, the journey from home to university is very different.

For one of us, this journey begins early in the morning, 2 hours before the first lecture. For others, it is only a matter of minutes. For others, it depends on external factors that he can't influence.

One of us does it by bike, another by train, another by public transport, and another on foot. Of course, there are people who decide not to attend lectures, but we will not talk about them, we will not talk about the bad, because that is not the purpose of this page, let's talk about the positive, about my way from home to university.

What to expect from it, maybe there are some obstacles? You'll find out very soon and get into my skin to find out what this path is from your point of view.
