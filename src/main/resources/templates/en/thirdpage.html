Step Three: Dress Up

Since I've already sipped my coffee and packed my bag, I'm ready to get dressed so I can go to university.

My choice of wardrobe isn't great. I usually choose clothes that I feel comfortable in - jeans, a sweater and comfortable shoes.

And then, depending on the season, which is out the window, I choose whether I need an extra piece of clothing. Because we all know the saying that there is no inappropriate weather, there is just inappropriate clothing.

An interesting fact

If I had to create a diagram showing what colors are in my wardrobe, they wouldn't be very bright, because like all computers, my favorite colors are black, gray, and then maybe there are just a few colors that stand out.

We can even look at the shirts of the Faculty of Computer Science - they are black, from the very beginning, when we started studying at university, this color is with us.

Perhaps this black color also symbolizes the computer science student - a calm, balanced mind.
