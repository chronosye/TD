Step five: Go to the rest room

When we come to the university, we can go inside it.

But there is one important reminder, do not go up the middle stairs. Many stories have been heard that if a student of the University of Latvia comes to the university using the middle ladder, then the student does not graduate from the university.

I have heard this from both lecturers and students that there are people who have really come to almost to the end, but they haven't passed the last tests.

That's why I use the side stairs to get to the university door.

I leave my outdoor clothes in the wardrobe and go to the lounge.

The lounge is one of the honorable things for computers, it can be used to play various board games, watch hockey, as well as various other things.

But the main thing in all this is that you can play novus or table tennis. Table tennis is busy every break between lectures. Often there is even a delay, because everyone likes this activity so much. Small tournaments are often held where students compete against the student.
