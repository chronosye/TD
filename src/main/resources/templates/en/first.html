First step: make coffee

Coffee. One of the most important components of a computer science student is coffee. Coffee with milk, black coffee, espresso, or whatever coffee a person can imagine there, a computer comes to life and is able to give new inspiration to the next act. And I'm no exception. Coffee is also part of my day. And that is the first thing on the road today that I will tell you about. So I'll tell you how I make my coffee.

Needed ingredients

Milk

Although you could drink coffee without milk, this time I will allow you to use milk in your recipe.

Sugar

No sugar either. A sweeter life requires two cubes of sugar.

Water

Also with the water that mixes all this coffee magic together. Without it, no way.

Coffee

Without this ingredient, we could hardly call it coffee.

Preparation:

1) We take a mug, pour 2 teaspoons of coffee into it.

2) Bring water to a boil, add 2 cubes of sugar to a cup.

3) When the water has boiled, pour it so that it occupies 3/4 of the cup.

4) Add milk to the remaining 1/4 of the cup.

5) Coffee ready! Enjoy it!
